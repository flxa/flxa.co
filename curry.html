Hot Mumma's Curry

By Nicholas Fletcher

Well it will be soon right? in 24 simple steps :)

This will take some thinking and timing. Before you start make sure you have the followin on hand (you don't want to be looking later).

    Kitchen Items

  • Biggest saucepan
  • Biggest pot for boiling potatoes
  • Strainer for straining potatoes
  • Wooden spoon
  • Chopping board
  • Peeler
  • Sharp knife

Then make sure you have these ingredients.

    Ingredients

  • 2 Cups Jasmine rice
  • ~600gms Chicken
  • 5 medium Potatoes
  • 1 brown onion
  • 2 teaspoons garlic
  • 3 desert spoons curry paste
  • Salt/Pepper

OK, you ready?

    Cookin' Time

  1. Measure 2 cups rice into rice cooker pot
  2. Wash rice
  3. Add 2 cups water to rice and start rice cooker
  4. Boil a full jug water
  5. Add about 2 cups water to a pot and put on high gas to heat up pot.
  6. Peel potatoes
  7. Cut into curry sized peices
  8. Add to pot with all boiled water
  9. Heat saucepan with olive oil on low
  10. Slice onion
  11. Add onion, turn up medium
  12. Brown onion while cutting chicken into tiny peices (keep eye out and stir occasionally)
  13. By 1/4 to 1/2 way through cutting chicken onion should be brown
  14. Transfer to bowl, keep saucepan hot, moar oil
  15. Add half chicken, brown, transfer to bowl
  16. Add other half chicken brown, transfer to bowl, keep saucepan hot
  17. Add 2 teaspoons garlic by itself, stir once
  18. Add 2 desert spoons curry paste (heaped)
  19. Stir it around and keep nose clear (it will burn if you breath in over pan)
  20. Add onion and chicken to curry, stir for 3 mins and fully coat in paste
  21. Drain potatoes, add to pan (whatever fits)
  22. Carefully stir in potatoes (dont break em up)
  23. Pour cream over curry and stir in well
  24. Serve
